Bridging the Information Gap in India's Power Grid

While official data exists, local power cuts often go untracked in real-time. Citizens rely on fragmented WhatsApp groups or simply waiting in the dark. BijliMap centralizes this information into a single, accessible map.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, community-first electricity information. We believe transparent data empowers rural and urban citizens alike to plan their daily lives effectively.

Our Vision

To build a nationwide electricity intelligence platform, scaling from village-level reporting to metro-city coverage with deep data analytics.
